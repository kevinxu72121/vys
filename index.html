Vancouver Youth Society

In the heart of Vancouver's dynamic landscape, a convergence of exceptional young minds finds its center within the unobtrusive embrace of the Vancouver Youth Society (VYS). VYS provides a haven for youth - aged between 14 to 25 years old - who personify excellence across a multitude of pursuits. Here, an air of intrigue shrouds a community bound by shared aspirations and limitless potential.

Vancouver Youth Society uncovers a realm where conventional paths are transformed into extraordinary journeys. Its members, originating from diverse backgrounds, share an innate spark that distinguishes them. Amid a world filled with clamor, VYS stands as a tribute to the artistry of understated achievement. These young individuals epitomize commitment, discipline, and fervor, each carving their distinctive paths with poise and precision.

Embracing an aura of enigma, VYS is a tapestry woven with threads of subtlety. Within this enclave, members find solace in shared passions, gaining the freedom to explore, learn, and innovate surrounded by a group of other similar minded youth.

VYS holds dinners once a month, allowing its members to converse with each other. This provides the perfect environment to meet other bright minded youth who may become valuable friends. Monthly dinners are hosted at many of Vancouver's esteemed venues, where elegance converges with innovation. Typically, dinners are held in the penthouse atop the Shangri-La Vancouver. Other venues, however, are also hosting sites for VYS dinners; the celebrated Mott 32, for instance, holds dinners for VYS.

From talented artists and programmers to entrepreneurs and scholars, VYS members present each other with insightful thoughts and abilities. Membership in the Vancouver Youth Society is not demarcated solely by achievements; it is an invitation extended to those who resonate with its ethos. If you find harmony in the confluence of humility and brilliance, if your drive emanates from the pursuit of excellence rather than the allure of recognition, then the Vancouver Youth Society might be the haven you seek.
