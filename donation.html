Donation

The Vancouver Youth Society is dedicated to fostering the growth and development of talented youth across Vancouver; your support can make a significant difference. By exposing talented young people to other similar minded and aged peers, aspiring youth are put into an environment fostering growth. By donating, you directly contribute to shaping a brighter future for Vancouver, helping its future leaders embrace their potential.

Your donation to the Vancouver Youth Society is an investment in the aspirations and success of the next generation. In order to keep Vancouver Youth Society accessible to anyone who is talented in their specialization, VYS always attempts to maintain its lowest possible membership fee. However, since the Vancouver Youth Society holds large dinners and gatherings monthly, donations help in our continued success, development, and expansion. Donations can be made through the PayPal link below. However, for larger donations, you may contact us if you would like an alternative platform for your transaction. All emails regarding donations can be directed to michael.xie@vancouveryouthsociety.ca.
