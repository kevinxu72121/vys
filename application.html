Membership

Amidst the busy downtown of Vancouver, Vancouver Youth Society (VYS) presents an opportunity for talented youth to meet and work with one another. Here, a sense of intrigue envelops a community fueled by shared aspirations and limitless potential.

VYS unveils a world where conventional paths transform into exceptional journeys. Members share an innate spark, embodying commitment, discipline, and fervor as they carve their unique paths.

With monthly dinners at esteemed venues such as the Shangri-La Vancouver's Penthouse or Mott 32, members of VYS are provided an opportunity to further expand their horizon of abilities and friends. Within the collective embrace of accomplishments, VYS members continue to recognize and improve on different aspects.

Membership in VYS extends an invitation to those who resonate with its ethos - a harmony of humility and brilliance. As twilight descends upon Vancouver, VYS becomes a sanctuary where potential flourishes, achievements are humbly acknowledged, and connections transcend the superficial. In this haven, excellence finds its home, redefining success on an odyssey exclusively presented by the Vancouver Youth Society.

Application Process

The application for Vancouver Youth Society (YVS) is open for any youth between ages 14 and 25. Although it is optional, we suggest that you have a current Youth Society Member write a letter of recommendation for you.

Following the submission of your application, if your abilities and values align with that of the YVS, you will be offered a dinner with VYS hosts; this serves more as a conversation than an interview. We encourage everyone interested to apply - the YVS application is open year long, with members being accepted on a rolling basis.
